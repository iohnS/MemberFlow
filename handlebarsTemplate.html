Lecture Committee

Are you interested in politics, economics or culture in the East Asian region? Then the Lecture Committee might be for you. As a member of the Lecture Committee you get the chance to reach out to politicians, scholars as well as other interesting people that have something to share about the region. In the committee you will get to plan lectures and together you will help come up with ideas on different topics that you find interesting. Want to learn more? Contact Lecture@asialund.org to get more information!

Event Committee

The Event Committee organizes a wide range of events that relate to East Asia. From traditional student sittnings to movie nights, language cafés, festivals, mahjong nights or events that center around tasting and cooking Asian food! If you have an idea of an event or want to help organize events don’t hesitate to reach out, you can reach the Heads of Events through events@asialund.org to get more information.

PR-Committee

The PR-Committee is in charge of the associations social media channels, being part of the committee you will learn more about how to structure posts and create a flow of information. As a part of the committee you also have the opportunity to develop your skills when it comes to photography and other kinds of graphic design. Contact PR@asialund.org for more information!

Become a writer for The Lunar Times

The Lunar Times is our online magazine focused on East and South-East Asia, which aims at raising the awareness of the most significant aspects of the region and its strategic importance. The Lunar Times targets the student population in Lund and acts as a gateway to Asia’s multifaceted political, economic, social and cultural realities. The Lunar Times is always looking for more contributors, for more information visit https://thelunartimes.net/, contact hej@thelunartimes.net or check here  https://thelunartimes.net/become-a-contributor/for more information! 

Become a Travel Coordinator

Every year EASA goes on study trips with a focus on Asia. In the past EASA has been to Japan, Taiwan and Stockholm. We are always planning more trips, keep a look out for openings to become a Travel Coordinator on our Social media!
